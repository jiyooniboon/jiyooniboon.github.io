### Why RainbowTrout
A few years ago I booted up my 10+ years old DS and played Animal Crossing Wild World. When I was little, I never understood fishing or bug catching. Honestly, thinking back on it, I don't even remember what I did in-game. But I loved it, and I loved re-discovering the game 10 years later. My grown-up self was catching all the fish and all the bugs and cleaning up the town and harrassing villagers and buying turnips and doing the NPC missions. I don't know why, but I remember going near the waterfall and catching the rainbow trout and making a lot of money. (I just checked the wiki and it isn't even worth that much. Maybe I just like that it's named rainbow.) Anyway, I just like rainbow trout as a name. Later I met one of my friends and she had a rainbow trout shirt and instead of thinking "a fish" I just immediately said "Omigosh you have a rainbow trout shirt!" and I think that's when I realized I like rainbow trout. They're colorful. I guess this isn't really an explaination for "why rainbow trout."
